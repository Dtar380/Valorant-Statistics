METHOD

The study had to fulfill three key points: data collection, data analysis, and data presentation.

The data was collected through a Google Forms survey, in which players were asked about their age, occupation, gender, and peak rank. This method relies heavily on players' honesty, so the data cannot be guaranteed to be 100% accurate.

The data analysis was simplified to avoid implicit bias that I might have when choosing the data. As a result, the presented data is very generic.

Data presentation is the reason for this study: to make the information accessible in a simple and interactive way. By giving users access to the data and the ability to make queries, they are empowered to draw their own conclusions.
